Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

Your Privacy Matters: At IAE Tutorials, we are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you register for IAE Tutorials, we collect:

  • Full name and username
  • Email address
  • Selected JAMB subjects
  • Exam performance data (scores, answers)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Providing and improving our CBT platform
  • Tracking your exam progress and generating results
  • Displaying your scores on the leaderboard (with your consent)
  • Communicating important platform updates

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Password hashing using Werkzeug security
  • Secure session management
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security updates

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only accessible to:

  • You (through your account dashboard)
  • Platform administrators (for support purposes)

5. Cookies

We use essential session cookies to keep you logged in and maintain your exam progress. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Opt-out of leaderboard display

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@iaetutorials.com.
